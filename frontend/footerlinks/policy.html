Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What We Collect

When you create an account on Code Bloom, we collect the information you provide during sign-up, such as your name, email address, and password. Your password is stored securely and is never visible to us in plain text. We may also store basic account activity, such as your course progress, once that feature is available.

Cookies

Code Bloom uses cookies to keep you signed in and to remember your session while you use the site. These cookies are necessary for login to work and are not used to track you across other websites.

How We Use Your Data

We use your account information to let you log in, keep your session active, and, in the future, track your progress through courses. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

This site may load fonts, icons, or other resources from third-party providers. These providers may collect basic technical data, such as your browser type, as part of normal web delivery.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your account data at any time by contacting us.
